Flick Soccer

Swipe Detection

This was the most important part of the making the game because it enables the user to interact with it. First the users finger was detected and assigned a variable, the velocity and length of the swipe until the finger is lifted fom the screen are the determining factors of how fast and high the ball travels in the game.

Goal Sensors

In order to get the goal sensors working properly I needed to put an interactive plane inside the net that would trigger a reaction on contact. The next step was to add little sensors in the corners that give an extra point if they come into contact with the ball.

Design and Textures
