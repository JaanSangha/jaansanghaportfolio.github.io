Getting Coins

The coin system is the currency used in game to make upgrades and develop your base. More currencies could easily be added like "wood" or "metal". You simply tap the screen to gain 1 coin at the start but that can be upgraded to yield more per touch.

Building Upgrades

Just like super popular building games there will be many different buildings that each provide different effects.There will be a main building that players can upgrade that will allow them to build a wider variety of things. Other buildings will generate currency over time and enable specific research upgrades to be done. Eventually adding a building with the ability to train combat units is in the plans.
